About Me

I am Rakshitha S, a passionate Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) graduate from Bangalore. With a strong foundation in full-stack web development, embedded systems, and IoT, I aim to build innovative solutions to real-world engineering challenges.

Currently working as a Trainee Web Developer at Siva Complete Solutions Pvt Ltd, I have hands-on experience building SEO-friendly and responsive websites using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript. I’ve also implemented SEO and SMO strategies using tools like Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager.

I completed an internship at Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL), where I collaborated with cross-functional teams and gained insights into military-grade defense technologies.

My projects include a Piezo Power Generation System and an Object Sorting System using Machine Learning, one of which was published in a SCOPUS-indexed journal. I also secured 4th place in the AI x SpaceTech Hackathon hosted by GalaxEye, where I worked on SAR to EO image translation using Pix2Pix GAN.
